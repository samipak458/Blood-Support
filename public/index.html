What is this all about ?

We solve the problem of blood emergencies by connecting blood donors and patients directly with the blood banks or the nearest hospital.

What we do ?

We will bridge the gap between the patients, donors, blood banks, maternity centers and hospitals across Pakistan with an efficient and seamless process.

Innovative

It is an innovative approach to address global heath. We provide immediate access to blood donors. Connecting donors directly with patients.

Network

We are of the several community organizations working together as a network that responds to emergencies in an efficient manner.

Get notified

Connect works with network partners to connect blood donors directly with people in blood need through an automated email and a message service.

Save Life

We are a non profit foundation and Our main objective is to make sure that everything is done to protect vulnerable persons. Help us by making a gift!

Totally Free

Our ultimate goal is to provide an easy -to-use, easy-to-access, fast, efficient, and reliable way to get life-saving blood, totally Free of cost.
